Description

This fresh, flavorful salad is served over a bed of quinoa and mixed vegetables. It’s topped with chopped cucumber, kalamata olives, cherry tomatoes, green beans, and feta cheese. Sprouted almonds are layered on to provide texture and crunch. 

The salad is dressed with a lemon oregano vinaigrette adding a nice citrus flavor.
