Southfork in the News

Southfork is the answer to that anxiety-inducing question of “what’s for lunch”? We offer a curated menu of three items that rotate daily in partnership with the city’s top chefs and food providers. Our meals are made with the highest quality ingredients—locally and sustainably sourced whenever possible.

It’s healthy. It’s convenient. It’s delivered right to your office—hassle free.
