Mission

Southfork is on a mission to change the way you experience lunch.

We make lunch easy, delivering delicious meals conveniently to your desk. Through partnerships with local chefs and restaurants, Southfork gives you access to some of the best food the city has to offer, made with the highest-quality ingredients.

We work with our partners to craft a curated menu of three tasty meal options that rotate daily. So whether you’re time strapped, bored with nearby options, or looking for something guilt-free, we are here to help you #LoveLunch.

  • Chef-Crafted

    We partner with leading food artisans to offer three curated lunch options every day.

  • Artisanal & Delicious

    Each meal is made with high-quality ingredients, locally sourced whenever possible.

  • Delivered to You

    We make lunch easy with delivery directly to your office.

We are currently delivering to select offices in Atlanta, with plans to roll out across the Southeast in the near future.

Our Story

Southfork was founded by Andy Blechman. Andy spent much of his career working on various problems in the food system. While living in NYC for more than 8 years, where anything and everything was accessible at any hour of the day, eating healthy and supporting a sustainable food system became more and more important to him. After moving to Atlanta with his wife, he realized that a lot of his friends weren’t eating well during the work day, simply because it wasn’t convenient or easy to do so. They were ordering fast food, because there weren’t other delivery options, or they were eating processed snacks at their desk, because they didn’t have time to deal with traffic in between meetings, or they were forgetting to eat at all.

Southfork was founded to change the way people experience lunch, by bringing restaurant-quality meals made with love by local chefs directly to offices.
